How it Works

Battle Arena, the best Esports Tournament App, let's see how it works?

Sign Up into Battle Areana App

Register with Email/Phone or Social Network. Get Joining Bonus upto ₹ 25.00 /-.

Participate in The Tournaments

Select your favourite game, and check ongoing tournament. Matches can be of Paid or Free, select your suitable match. You may need to enter your in-game id for identification.

Join the match

Note the scheduled time of Match, Check in to the app before 5 mins to get Room ID and Password of the match, if required.
